Ridge cap repointing is critical roof maintenance that many homeowners overlook. Cracked or crumbling mortar between ridge tiles lets water in and can cause tiles to dislodge in high winds. Smart Choice replaces old rigid mortar with flexible pointing compounds that move with your roof.

## The Process
1. Remove all old, deteriorated mortar from ridge caps and gable ends
2. Clean the area thoroughly
3. Apply flexible bedding compound
4. Finish with colour-matched flexible pointing
5. Final inspection and clean-up

## Why Flexible Pointing?
Traditional cement mortar cracks within a few years as your roof naturally expands and contracts. Our flexible pointing compounds are designed for Australian conditions — they flex with the roof, preventing future cracks and leaks.
