Roof valleys are the channels where two roof planes meet — and they're one of the most common sources of roof leaks. Smart Choice provides expert roof valley replacement using quality materials and proper installation techniques.

## Signs You Need Valley Replacement
- Water stains on ceilings below valley lines
- Visible rust or corrosion on valley iron
- Debris buildup that can't be cleared
- Previous patch repairs that keep failing
- Roof age over 20 years with original valleys

## Our Approach
We remove the old valley iron, inspect the underlying structure, install new Colorbond or galvanised valley iron with proper fall, and reseal all surrounding tiles. Every valley replacement includes a water test before we leave.
