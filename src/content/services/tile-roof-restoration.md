Smart Choice Roof Restorations specialises in comprehensive tile roof restoration across Melbourne. Our proven process brings your roof back to life — protecting your home and boosting kerb appeal.

## What's Included
- High-pressure water cleaning to remove dirt, moss, and lichen
- Full roof inspection and assessment
- Replacement of all cracked, broken, or slipped tiles
- Professional roof repointing (flexible pointing compound)
- Application of premium sealer and two-coat paint system
- All valleys, ridge caps, and flashings checked and replaced as needed

We use only premium Dulux or Nutech coatings with a 10-year manufacturer warranty. Every restoration includes a full written report with before and after photos. No shortcuts — just quality workmanship you can trust.
