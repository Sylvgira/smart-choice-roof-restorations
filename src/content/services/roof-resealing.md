Over time, the sealants on your roof break down — leading to leaks, damp, and structural damage. Smart Choice provides thorough roof resealing that creates a watertight barrier protecting your home.

## What We Seal
- Ridge caps (bedding and pointing)
- Roof valleys and valley irons
- Flashings around chimneys, vents, and skylights
- Parapet wall cappings
- Expansion joints and roof penetrations
- Box gutters and internal gutters

## Materials
We use flexible, UV-stable sealants that move with your roof through Melbourne's temperature extremes — preventing the cracking and separation that causes leaks.
