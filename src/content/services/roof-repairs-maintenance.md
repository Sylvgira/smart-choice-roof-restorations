Leaks, broken tiles, damaged flashings — roof problems don't wait, and neither do we. Smart Choice provides fast, honest roof repairs and preventative maintenance across Melbourne.

## Common Repairs We Handle
- Leaking roof diagnosis and repair
- Broken, cracked, or slipped tile replacement
- Damaged or corroded flashing replacement
- Valley iron repair and replacement
- Ridge cap re-bedding and repointing
- Storm damage assessment and emergency repairs
- Sagging roof structure assessment

## Our Approach
We don't just patch the problem. Every repair starts with a thorough inspection to identify the root cause. We provide honest advice — if a full restoration would be more cost-effective long-term, we'll tell you.
