A dirty roof isn't just an eyesore — moss, lichen, and accumulated debris can trap moisture and accelerate deterioration. Smart Choice provides professional high-pressure roof cleaning that's thorough, safe, and effective.

## What It Removes
- Built-up dirt and grime
- Moss, lichen, and algae growth
- Leaf litter and organic debris
- Pollution staining
- Rust stains and metal runoff marks

## Safe for Your Roof
We use adjustable pressure settings appropriate for your roof type — high enough to clean effectively, but controlled to avoid tile damage or water ingress. All downpipes are checked for blockages during the process.
