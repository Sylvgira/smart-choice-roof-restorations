Metal roofs are durable but they still need professional care. Smart Choice restores Colourbond, Zincalume, and galvanised metal roofs across Melbourne with a specialist process designed for Australian conditions.

## What's Included
- Full high-pressure clean to remove all surface contaminants
- Rust inspection and treatment of all affected areas
- Replacement of corroded screws and fasteners
- Application of premium metal primer on treated areas
- Full respray using industry-grade metal roof coatings
- Gutter and flashing integrity check

## Best For
Colourbond homeowners wanting to refresh their roof colour without the cost of a full roof replacement. Also ideal for older galvanised roofs needing protective recoating.
