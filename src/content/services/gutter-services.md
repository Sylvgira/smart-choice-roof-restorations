Blocked, damaged, or rusted gutters are a leading cause of home water damage. Smart Choice provides comprehensive gutter services — from simple cleaning to complete replacement — keeping Melbourne homes protected.

## Services
- **Gutter Cleaning:** Manual removal of all debris followed by flushing to ensure free flow
- **Gutter Repairs:** Fixing leaks at joints, replacing rusted sections, re-securing loose brackets
- **Gutter Replacement:** Full replacement with new Colorbond or galvanised gutters to match your home
- **Downpipe Services:** Clearing blockages, replacing damaged sections

## Why It Matters
A blocked gutter overflows onto walls and foundations. In Melbourne's wet months, this can cause serious structural damage. Regular gutter maintenance is the cheapest insurance for your home.
