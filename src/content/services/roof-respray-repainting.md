A roof respray is one of the most cost-effective ways to transform your home's appearance. Smart Choice uses only premium paint systems designed for the harsh Australian climate.

## What's Included
- Full roof clean and surface preparation
- Application of matching or new colour coating
- Two-coat system for maximum durability
- All ridge caps, valleys, and flashings painted to match
- Clean-up of all overspray and site protection

## Colour Options
We work with the full Dulux and Colorbond colour ranges. Whether you want to match your existing colour or go for a complete refresh, we'll help you choose the right shade for your home's style.
